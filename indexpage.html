Welcome to this website!

I'm Thijs Carrière. Currently I'm a second year research Master's student, following the programme Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences at Utrecht University.

In this programme I learned all about the best statistical techniques and state of the art methodology of research in the social sciences. Before this Masters, I did my Bachelors in psychology. These two combined prepare me to be able to add my share to research.









About me

I'm 24 years old and was born and raised in Zeist, a village near Utrecht, where I'm currently still living with my boyfriend. After highschool, I started studying psychology at Utrecht University with the intention of becoming a therapist. During this Bachelors, I found out how interesting and fun statistics and research are, and I decided to follow more courses on it. Because I found statistics so interesting, I decided to follow the research Masters programme 'Methodology and Statistics for the Behavioural, Biomedical and Social Sciences'. I'm currently finishing this Masters and starting to look forward to start working in research.

I've always been a broadly interested person, and within statistics, this is no difference. My main interests are on psychometrics and structural equation modeling, which are topics related to my Master Thesis.
